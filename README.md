# Saarthi

**Peer-to-peer parcel delivery and travel-companion matching for India.** Saarthi connects people who need something delivered — or someone accompanied — with verified travellers already making that exact journey by flight or train.

**Live Prototype Link -**  https://siddharthmall.github.io/Saarthi/


## The problem

Millions of people travel between Indian cities every day with spare capacity in their bags. At the same time, sending a parcel intercity is slow and expensive, and families worry about elderly relatives or minors travelling alone. Saarthi turns everyday journeys into a trusted delivery and companionship network — cheaper than courier services, faster than post, and warmer than sending a loved one off by themselves.

## What it does

Saarthi serves two personas in one app:

- **Send** — a requester posts a parcel (or a companion request for a family member), picks a verified traveller on their route, and pays into escrow. Money is only released when the handover is confirmed.
- **Travel** — a traveller publishes an upcoming trip, accepts requests that fit, carries or accompanies, and gets paid instantly on handover.

Every handover is secured by a **4-digit code** and a **geo-stamped proof-of-delivery photo**, so both sides are protected end to end.

## Key features

- **Escrow-backed payments** — funds are held safely and released only on a confirmed handover.
- **Saarthi Ticket** — every booking issues a boarding-pass-style ticket with route, traveller, and handover code.
- **Live journey tracking** — a real-time tracker showing pickup → in transit → landed → delivered.
- **Handover code + proof photo** — geo-stamped, time-stamped verification at drop-off.
- **Travel-companion mode** — background-checked travellers accompany elderly passengers or minors, with ID-verified handovers and strict privacy (no photos of the person).
- **Multi-handover trips** — travellers can carry up to two handovers per journey, sequenced one at a time.
- **Item protection & insurance tiers**, in-app notifications, masked chat, and a full trips history.

## Tech
